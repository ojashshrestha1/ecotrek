Eco Friendly Tour and Trekking Agency in Nepal

Ecological Treks Pvt. Ltd is a Trekking and Tour Operator Agency in Nepal which has been run by pioneers of tourism sectors of the country. Our team led by one of the most active tourism and social pioneer in Nepal, Mr. Ramesh Dhamala who founded the company under the philosophy of People First and Customer Oriented.
As a chairman of the Ecological Treks, he has been dedicated himself in promoting Eco-tourism and create community awareness, exploring and plan for new trekking route, cooperating with INGOs and schools to conduct voluntary service in the field of education, medical care and cultural exchange in the remote areas.

    Trekking from Jiri, also known as the Switzerland of Nepal,to Everest Base Camp is an astounding and pleasant experience in the earth. Everest Base Camp Trek is one of the most popular trekking trails in the world.

    Annapurna Base Camp Trekking is one of the most enthralling trekking routes of Nepal that takes you to the foot of the Mt. Annapurna which is at the altitude of 4210m..

    Ghorepani Poon Hill Trekking is the shortest and exhilarating trek in the Annapurna Region Trekking. It is the easier trek in the Annapurna Region which can be done within a week. One gets opportunity to view the panorama of the mountains like Annapurna (8091m), Machhapuchchhre(6997m)...

    Langtang Gosainkunda Helambu Trek is a circular trek around the Langtang region that combines the Langtang valley, Gosainkunda and Helambu trek. The main attractions of this trek are Langtang valley, Helambu valley and Holy Lakes of Gosainkunda.
