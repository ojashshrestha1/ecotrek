Tours in India

India has very vast landscape- from the Himalayas in the north to the coastal south, from humid deltas in the east to deserts in the west. It has several beautiful places which has always attracts the visitors around the world. Over land India tour is impossible in short time period it takes very long time. Ecological trek offer India tour package by including some of its important places such as New Delhi, Agra, Jaipur, Udaipur and Goa. India tour package starts with exploring the New Delhi where you will visit the UNESCO World Heritage QutabMinar, the world's tallest brick minaret and the 20th-century Birla Temple, which honors the Hindu goddess of wealth. After visiting the New Delhi tour continue to Agra this place is famous for TajMahal. TajMahal was built by Emperor Shah Jahan as a mausoleum for his beloved Queen MumtajMahal. This monumental construction, which took 22 years to complete is most famous for its imposing white-marble dome, and is considered the finest example of Mughal architecture in the world. In Agra you will visit to Agra Fort, the original red sandstone fort built by Mughal Emperor Akbar in 1565. The Fort's maze of courtyards, marble mosque, and lavish private chambers reflect the grandeur of the Mughal Empire. After visiting Agra you will go to Jaipur where you will visit the 16th century Amber Fort. Ascend the hill to the fort by jeep or on the back of an elephant if available and admire views of Jaipur original city walls and the Maotha Lake below. Other important places of Jaipur for visit are Maharaja Jaisingh, the museum at the Maharaja City Palace and shopping stop at Jaipur Handicrafts & Carpets market. From Jaipur you will go to Udaipur where you will visit the City Palace, Jagdish Temple, and Saheliyonki Bari and you will enjoy a boat ride on Lake Pichola. From Udaipur you will finally reach to Goa which is the last destination of this tour package. Goa was ruled by the Portuguese for over 450 years, the Portuguese-influenced state is renowned for its world heritage architecture, places of worship, and beaches you may choose to spend few times in Panjim, the state capital of Goa.

Panjim is a attractive city on the left bank of the Mandovi River that has beautiful Latin-styled red-tiled roofed houses, well laid gardens, cobbled streets, statues, and avenues lined with trees, visit the expansive old Portuguese Fort Aguada with its four storey lighthouse, the oldest of its kind in Asia, Dona Paula, and the notable Churches in Old Goa - a UNESCO Word Heritage Site. In Goa you will enjoy on the beautiful beaches and head to the commercial capital of Margao to visit its grand colonial mansions, the Closed Market, municipal building, garden, and the numerous churches and temples.
