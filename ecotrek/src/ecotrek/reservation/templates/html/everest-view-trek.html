Everest View Trek - 13 Days Days

Everest Region, also known as Khumbu Region, is one of the most famous trekking regions all over the world. The shortest trek in the Everest region is the Everest View Trek. The trek to view the Mount Everest that presides over a geographical and cultural contrast offers a wide range of natural and cultural encounters. It offers the superb view of the Mount Everest and a chance to explore the culture and lifestyle of the Sherpa people. The trail passes through Sagarmatha National Park with pine forests, rhododendron flowers and different types of wildlife, village of Tengboche which lies at the altitude of 3930m, highest Tengboche Buddhist monastery (4100m) etc.

With this trek, trekkers can capture the astonishing views of the highest mountain of the world Mt. Everest along with other 8000m high mountains without ascending the high altitude. This trek is best for those who wish to experience the region and witness the panoramic view of the great Himalayas by their own eyes but are unable to reach Everest Base Camp and Kalapathar. In this trail moderate type of climate is found and suitable for all type of people. The highest place star hotel of the world Hotel Everest View form where close-up view of the Everest is possible, is the attraction of this trek. This is the shortest trek in the Everest Region. This journey is accomplished within two weeks. So, one can take the experience of this trekking in holiday also. The trail of this trek begins from Lukla which is 30 minutes flight from Kathmandu and trail ascent to Phakding, Jorsale, Namche Bazar, KhumJung and reaches Tengpoche. In Namche bazaar Phakding, Jorsale and their near village you know the Sherpa community and their unique culture.

Everest View Trek Itinerary

Day 01: Arrive in Kathmandu (1310 m) and transfer to hotel.
Day 02: Kathmandu sightseeing tour.
Day 03: Fly to Lukla and trek to Phakding (2640m), 30 minutes flight and 3 hours of walk.
Day 04: Phakding to Namche Bazar (3440m), 5/6 hours of walk.
Day 05: Acclimatization day at Namche Bazzar(3440m).
Day 06: Namche Bazaar to Thame (3800m) , 6/7 hours of walk.
Day 07: Trek to Khumjung (3780m)6/7 hours of walk.
Day 08: Trek to Tengboche (3860m), 6/7 hours of walk.
Day 09: Explore around Tengboche.
Day 10: Trek to Namche, 6/7 hours of walk.
Day 11: Namche Bazaar to Lukla, 6/7 hours of walk.
Day 12: Fly to Kathmandu, free day to relax or shopping.
Day 13: Depart to your own destination.
