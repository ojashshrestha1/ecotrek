Trekking Information

Trekking is the long difficult journey, especially made on foot. Nowadays trekking has become very popular and authentic source to have the knowledge of culture and tradition of different region and people. Trekking liberate the people from their daily hassle and tussle with aesthetic pleasure of nature and culture. Trekkers come to Nepal to see the mountains and walk in the hilly regions untouched by the modern developments. In Nepal there are two different ways of trekking: Tea House Trekking and Camp Trekking.

Trekking can be accomplished throughout the year in Nepal.
