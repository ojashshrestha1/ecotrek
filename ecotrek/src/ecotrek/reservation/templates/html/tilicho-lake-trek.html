Tilicho Lake Trek

The challenging Trekking to Tilicho Lake (4919m.), the highest lake of the world, is a wonderful Summer Trek in the Annapurna Region. Tilicho Lake is also considered as the sacred Lake by the Hindu people as it is mentioned in the Ramayana- the holy book of the hindus, and many Hindu pilgrims visit this place every year. It receives the entire glacial melt of the northern slope of Annapurna Range as well as the Thorung Peak. Tilicho Lake Trekking is the adventurous and exciting trekking in the isolated and rugged trail of Annapurna Region which offers the majestic vistas of the mountains like Annapurna, Machhapuchhre, Manaslu, Tilicho peak etc. During the trek you enjoy the culture and tradition of different ethnic people which is similar to the Tibetan people, views of river gorges, beautiful Marsyangdi Valley, centuryâ€™s old Buddhist monastery. The pristine natural beauty and dense forest with wildlife as well as the hospitality of the people added further pleasure to the trekkers.

Tilicho Lake Trek commences from Besisahar of Lamjung District which is the starting point of other Annapurna Region Trekking. From Besisahar you walk along the bank of Marsyangdi River enjoying the panorama of Manaslu and Himal Chuli to the east as well as other mountains. In route you cross the beautiful villages inhabited by a wide diversity of people from different ethnic groups with the spectacular views of the white Himalayas. After you reach the Tilicho Lake there are two routes to return: either you can cross the challenging pass of Moesokanta La Pass to Jomsom or travel to Thorung La pass. To get full enjoyment of Tilicho Lake Trek, the best time is beginning of March to end of May and beginning of September to end of December.

Day to Day Itinerary

Day 01: Arrive at Kathmandu and transfer to hotel
Day 02: Kathmandu Sightseeing day
Day 03: Drive to Beshisahar and By local jeep to Bulbule.
Day 04: Trek to Jagat, 5/6 hours walk.
Day 05: Trek to Dharapani, 5/6 hours walk.
Day 06: Trek to Chame (2670m.), 5/6 hours walk.
Day 07: Trek to Pisang (3200m.), 5/6 hours walk.
Day 08: Trek to Manang (3540m.), 5/6 hours walk.
Day 09: Rest day at Manang village
Day 10: Trek to Khangsar (3734m.), 3/4 hours walk.
Day 11: Trek to Tilicho Base camp (4140m.), 5/6 hours walk.
Day 12: Trek back to Manang village (3540m.), 5/6 hours walk
Day 13: Back to Dhukur Pokhari, 5/6 hours walk.
Day 14: Back to Timang, 5/6 hours walk.
Day 15: Back to Jagat, 5/6 hours walk
Day 16: Back to Bulbule.
Day 17: Drive back to Pokhara and Fly back to Kathmandu or drive back direct to Kathmandu.
Day 18: Farewell day
