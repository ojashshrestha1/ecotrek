Trekking in Nepal

Trekking in Nepal takes you very close to the natural landscape and gives the chance to explore the culture and the people of mountain range in Nepal. Himalayas are taken as the best trekking destination in all over the world. As Nepal is a dwelling of eight of the world’s highest mountain including the world’s highest Mount Everest, one can experience the Shangri-La in Nepal. The mountain ranges and the hilly topography of Nepal offer breathtaking, enthralling trekking routes. Some of the popular trekking trails in Nepal are Annapurna Trekking Trails, Everest Trekking Trails, Langtang Trekking Trails, Kanchenjunga base camp trek, Makalu base camp trek, Manaslu trek, and Upper Dolpo trek.

Trekking in Nepal is an unforgettable experience as it not only let you to experience the unexplored trails made by nature, but also bring the visitor directly in touch with the country and its people. It also helps the visitors to experience and appreciate the unique culture of Nepal like in Annapurna base camp trekking you can know the unique culture of Gurung community in Ghandruk. While walking on the foothill trails of the mountain, you can encounter the natural beauty and thinks that it is the real heaven.

Though Nepal is very small in size, it is vast in natural, religious, geographical and cultural diversity which fascinates the trekkers. Trekking in Nepal is one of the famous adventure sports awaiting the trekking enthusiast in Nepal. In Nepal trekking enthusiast can also explore the rugged beauty of the lofty mountains romancing the shimmering waterfalls cascading down in glee like in Kanchenjunga base camp trekking, Annapurna base camp trekking, Langtang Gosainkunda trekking etc.

In Nepal, trekking can be combined with various other adventure options and activities such as sightseeing, mountain biking, rock climbing, bungee-jumping, paragliding etc. for example one can enjoy bungee-jumping,Rolwaling trek, paragliding in Annapurna region, trekking in Pokhara etc.

Trekking is the long difficult journey, especially made on foot.
