Annapurna View Trek

Annapurna View Trek is short, easy and romantic trek in the Annapurna Region. This trek is suitable for those who want to go trek with their family members, those who can’t to go high mountain trek but wish to see close views of several white capped peaks and for those who are the beginners of the trek. Annapurna View Trek take you to the most popular Gurung ethnic people’s villages Ghandruk and Ghorepani from where you can see the close views of the Annapurna and other different beautiful peaks such as Machhapuchhre, Dhaulagiri, Nilgiri, Manaslu, Buddha Himal, Himchuli, Ganesh Himal, Dhampus peak, Tukuche peak, Thorung peak and Tilicho peak. Humble and ever smiling Gurung people are very rich in their culture and festivals so during this trek you will get the opportunity to know their culture and to be participating in their festivals by tasting their varieties of local foods. In Ghandruk village for the pleasure to their new guests Gurung people organize dancing and singing program with their own Gurung traditional song, musical instrument and dress. You can sing and dance together with them and this enjoyable moment will be the most remarkable moment of your life.

Annapurna View Trek also take you to the Poon Hill which is the beautiful place to see sunrise and sunset views, beautiful landscapes, Rivers, villages and mountains. Annapurna View Trek starts with short drive from Pokhara to Phedi. From Phedi we will reach to Ghandruk after completing the romantic journey of Dhampus and Landruk. We will enjoy in the natural and cultural beauty of Ghandruk village. From Ghandruk we will go to Poon Hill through Tadapani and Ghorepani. After having the beautiful natural glimpse from Poon Hill, we will trek down to Nayapul through Ulleri. Trek expert of Ecological trek has designed Annapurna View Trek itineraries. We tried our best to include all the beautiful places and romantic activities in our itineraries. If you are interested to visit other places which are not included in our package we will redesign our Annapurna View Trek as per your request.

Day to Day Itinerary

Day 01: Arrive at Kathmandu and transfer to hotel.
Day 02: Kathmandu Sightseeing day.
Day 03: Drive or Flight to Pokhara.
Day 04: Trek to Tikhedhunga (1540m.).
Day 05: Trek to Ghorepani (2850m.)
Day 06: Trek to Ghandruk (1940m.).
Day 07: Trek to Pothana(1900m.)
Day 08: Trek back to Phedi and drive back to Pokhara.
Day 09: Fly/ drive back to Kathmandu.
Day 10: Farewell day
