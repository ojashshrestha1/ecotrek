Pokhara City Sightseeing

Pokhara City is the third largest city of Nepal with the beautiful landscape surrounded by mountains and lakes.It provides visitors with the beautiful view of mountain ranges and the lakes. Pokhara is the most important tourist(national as well as international) destinations in Nepal. The Pokhara city consists of various popular places such as Davis Falls, Mahendra Gufa,Bindabasini temple, Panchase Lake, World Peace Pagoda and many more.

The most exciting part of Pokhara City Sightseeing is tour to World Peace Pagoda. One feels very refreshed and relaxed seeing these places. As three of the tallest mountains view can be seen from the Pokhara city, it also provides a base for trekkers undertaking the Annapurna Base Camp.
