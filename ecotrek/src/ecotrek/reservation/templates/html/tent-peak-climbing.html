Tent Peak climbing - 20 Days

Tent Peak (5850m) which is also known as Tarphu Himal is located in the heart of Annapurna region with Mt. Annapurna. Jimmy Roberts nicknamed Tarphu Himal as ‘Tent Peak’ in 1956. Tent Peak Climbing takes you into the heart of Annapurna sanctuary from where you can enjoy the eye-catching view of the Annapurna range. Tent Peak Climbing is an opportunity to take the experience of high mountain expedition climbing at lower altitude. It combines both the peak climbing and the trekking. On the one hand you get the experience of mountain climbing and on the other hand you get the experience of trekking in the heart of Annapurna base camp with spectacular scenery of the snow capped mountains.

Tent Peak climbing and trekking like other Annapurna region trekking, too starts from Nayapul, around 100km north from the famous Tourists City of Pokhara. Pokhara which is also known as the Lake City, you can visit to different lakes, enjoy paragliding and boating. From Nayapul you ascend to the destination following bank of Modi Khola passing through green hills of Rhododendron Forest, terraced farms, beautiful waterfalls, fascinating Gurung and Magar villages etc. It allows you to enjoy the aesthetic beauty of the nature. This climbing and trekking allows you to have an insight into the culture and tradition of the Gurung and Magar community. From the Tent Peak Base Camp you climb the peak from the North-West Ridge route. From the summit of the Tent Peak you are amazed by the astonishing view of the gorgeous mountains Like Annapurna, Machhapuchhre, Hiunchuli, Nilgiri etc. So book your trip to Tent peak climbing today with us and put your foot on the mountain with less effort.
