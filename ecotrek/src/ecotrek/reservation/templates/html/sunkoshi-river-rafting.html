Sunkoshi River Rafting

Sunkoshi River Rafting is the longest River rafting offered in the Nepal which is about 270km.from Dolalghat to Chatara. Many people consider Sunkoshi River rafting to be one of the world’s 10 classic River journey. You can begin a River trip and end the trip looking down the hot and dusty plain area of the north India, just nine or 10 days later.

In Sunkoshi River Rafting, the rafters can take the pleasure of two kinds of water journey: high water and low water, and two class of international grading: 5 grade -5 grades. From the season of September to December, rafters can take the experience of 5 grade rafting. Similarly, from the season of January to May, rafters can take the pleasure of -5 grade rafting. But, from the season of June to August because of monsoon with heavy rain, water in Sunkoshi is very high as a consequence, Sunkoshi is not runable.

The Sunkoshi start off in a relaxed manner with class 2 and small class 3 rapids during the first couple of days. And on the third day, the rapids become more powerful and frequent, with high water trippers finding themselves astonished at just how big a wave in a River can get. While the lower section of large volume River are usually become flat, the Sunkoshi reserves some of its biggest and best rapids for the last days which ultimately helps to  keep the rafters pleasure even at the time of last moment.

The Sunkoshi River Rafting is the combination of white water, scenery, villages and truly quiet and an introspective evenings. Mingle of these things, easy approach from Kathmandu valley, experience of high and low water, rafting in two different grade makes Sunkoshi River Rafting most desirable to anyone. That's why it has become one of the World’s 10 Classic River Journey.

Itinerary

Day 01: Arrival in Kathmandu and transfer to Hotel
Day 02: Sightseeing of Kathmandu valley
Day 03: Drive from Kathmandu to Dolalghat, 3hrs and short rafting camp to TarangGaun
Day 04: Rafting from Tarang Gaun to Dumja Village, 4hrs
Day 05: Rafting from Dumja Village to Khurkot Gaun, 6hrs
Day 06: Rafting from Khurkot Village to Likhu Khola, 5hrs
Day 07: Rafting from Likhu Khola to Harkapur Village, 6hrs
Day 08: Rafting from Harkapur Village to Rai Ghat, 6hrs
Day 09: Rafting from Rai Ghat to Bander Ghat, 6hrs
Day 10: Rafting from Bander Ghat to Bonjur Ghat, 6hrs
Day 11: Rafting from Bonjur Ghat to Barahachhetra, 6hrs
Day 12: Rafting from Barahachhetra to Chatara (put out point), 2hrs
Day 13: Drive to Kathmandu or drive to Biratnagar to take flight back to Kathmandu
