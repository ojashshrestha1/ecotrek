Annapurna Circuit Biking Tour - 18 Days

Annapurna Circuit Biking Tour is the most adventurous and amusing biking tour of Nepal. This is the long biking tour around the Annapurna Mountain Region. Annapurna Circuit Biking Tour passed through the Lamjung, Manang, Mustang and Myagdi districts. Lamjung and Myagdi are located in the lower elevation with beautiful lush landscape. Manang and Mustang are located in higher elevation with beautiful snowy peaks and barren landscape. Annapurna Region offer the stunning glimpse of beautiful snowy peaks such as Annapurna, Machhapuchhre, Dhaulagiri, Nilgiri, Manaslu, Buddha Himal, Himchuli, Ganesh Himal, Dhampus peak, Tukuche peak, Thorung peak and Tilicho peak. The trail goes through the beautiful forest, falls, Lake, pristine River and artistic landscape which catches the eye and take the heart of the riders.

On the way, there are Brahmin, Chhetri, Gurung, Magar and Tibetan Buddhist villages on the way. These ethnic people are very rich in their culture and festivals so during this tour cyclists get opportunity to know their culture and participate in their festivals.
