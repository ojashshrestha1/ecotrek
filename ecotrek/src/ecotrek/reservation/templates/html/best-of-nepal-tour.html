Best of Nepal Tour

Best of Nepal Tour package combines the several important places of Nepal which will give diverse experience to the visitors. In this tour, visitors can get historical, cultural,religious ,natural, and wilder experiences. Kathmandu is culturally and historically important so in this tour historical and cultural places of Kathmandu such as Bashantapur Durbar Square, Patan Durbar Square, Bhaktapur Durbar Square, Pashupatinath, Boudhanath, Swayambhunath and Kirtipur are included. After visiting Kathmandu this tour will take you to Nagarkot. Nagarkot is naturally beautiful place which offer romantic view of sunrise and sun set, panoramic view of stunning mountains and glimpse of Kathmandu valley.

From Nagarkot, our  tour moves to Gorkha which is the important town of Nepal from where unification of Nepal began. In Gorkha you will visit historical Shah Palace which is located on the top of the hill direct ascends from Gorkha town. After visiting in Gorkha you will go to Manakamana temple which is also located in the Gorkha district. Manakamana is the one of the holiest temple of Hindu pilgrimage people. From Manakamana you will go to Bandipur village of Tanahu district. In this village different ethnics people are living so, diverse culture can be seen there. Along with this cultural attraction Bandipur is also famous for its natural attraction from where eye pleasing and breath taking scene and scenery can be seen. Lumbini is religiously important place because Lord Buddha was born here. As the birth place of Lord Buddha, Lumbini is considered foundation of the world peace. All the important places of Lumbini are included in this tour. While visiting in Lumbini you will get eternal solace. From Lumbini you will finally return to Kathmandu.
