Amadablam Expedition

Amadablam 6,812m is considered one of most beautiful Mountain in the world and expedition on Mt. Amadablam is very quixotic. Mountaineers who are seeking for romantic journey on mountain rather than risky and dangerous, Amadablam Expedition can definitely become their choice. Mt.Amadablam is steep rock mountain as a result journey to Mt. Amadablamis equally difficult as it is romantic. So, every climbers need to have an experience of steep rock climbing and cold ice walking. Your journey to Mt. Amadablam starts with the flight from Kathmandu to Lukla. After completing the five days trek of Namche, Tengboche and Pangboche you will reach to the Amadablam Base Camp. From the base camp you move up through old grassy moraine ridges passing through a saddle and then turning to north climbing up rocky ground and through boulders to the ridge where camp 1 is set up. From camp 1, you will cross a rocky bowl and climb the ridge frequently alternating the sides along the fixed lines to the camp 2. Climbing the ridge involves severe rock climbing which leads to the top of a yellow tower. The climbing route here from camp 2 changes significantly with the steep mixed ditch of rock, ice and snow. The route leads to the slope and climbs through steep snow and ice runnel, along the snow ridge to reach camp 3. The summit from camp 3 requires steep climb on snow and ice to the right of a huge lynching glacier. From the summit of Mt.
