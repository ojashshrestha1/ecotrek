Mountain Expedition in Nepal

Nepal is considered as the country of Himalayas, with its ten out of the world’s sixteen above 8000m icy peaks. Anyone who really wants to challenge the icy peaks then Nepal is the best destination for them. Nepal’s north part journey is the journey of paradise for the Mountain Expedition lover. Because of world’s ten highest peaks and other several white capped peaks Expedition in Nepal become most challenging, adventurous, popular and equally amusing sport. This sport became popular in Nepal when Sir Edmund Hillary from New Zealand and Tenzing Norgay Sherpa from Nepal first climbed the Mt. Everest in May 1953. As the Himalayas are the greatest mountains range of the world, their magnificence, enormity, charm and anonymity will always appeal for those who venture in to this region. Hence, flock of white peaks’ lovers increases every year in Nepal.

Peaks, 6000 to 8848m are available in Nepal for the expedition so Nepal has become dream land for the lover of Mountains Expedition. Mountaineers can choose romantic expedition like on Mt Amadablam and Barunste and most challenging expedition like Mt Sagarmatha and Mt Manaslu according to their interest. Other popular Mountains expedition in Nepal are Mt. Annapurna expedition, Mt. Lhotse expedition, Mt Dhaulagiri expedition, Mt. Pumori expedition, Mt. Baruntse expedition. Expedition on these different Mountains fills up with their own romantic, risky and adventurous feature. Several mountains and distinct feature of their own play the vital role to make Nepal as the last destination of mountaineers around the world

As the tallest mountain of the world Mt Everest Expedition is most challenging, courageous, adventurous and risky expedition of the world.
