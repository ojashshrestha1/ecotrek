Kathmandu Valley Bird Watching

Nepal is the best/ultimate destination for bird lovers. Kathmandu Valley Bird Watching Tour is the most popular destination for bird watching including Pachpokhari, Chitwan and Koshi Tappu Wildlife Reserve Camp .Kathmandu valley is surrounded on all sides by wonderfully green which are covered with dense forests. These hills and dense forest provide a safe home for various species of birds. Unique and different species of birds can be seen in Kathmandu valley. Kathmandu Valley Bird Watching Tour begins from Kathmandu and heading towards Godavari, we hike up to Phulchoki . Phulchoki offers a good amount of amazing birds and great views of the surrounding hills. We then head off to the Shivapuri Watershed and Wildlife Reserve where another great number of birds can be seen.

Itinerary

Day 01: Arrival in Airport and transfer to Hotel
Day 02: Bird watching in Godavari
Day 03: Bird watching in Phulchowki (2759m.)
Day 04: Bird watching in Shivapuri National Park
Day 05: Bird watching in Nagarjun
Day 06: Sightseeing in Kathmandu valley
Day 07: Transfer to Airport for final departure
