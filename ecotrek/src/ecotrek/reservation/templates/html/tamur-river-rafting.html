Tamur River Rafting

Tamur River is the sixth largest river in Nepal. Tamur River Rafting is one of the complete adventure trips in Nepal including the remote part of eastern Nepal with beautiful views of some biggest mountains in the world.  Tamur River Rafting actually starts with trekking with start point  as Basntapur and ending point as Dovan.

Day to Day Itinerary

Day 01: Arrival in Kathmandu Airport and transfer to Hotels
Day 02: Sightseeing in Kathmandu Valley
Day 03: 10 hrs dive or flight to Biratnagar and short drive to Basantapur
Day 04: Preparation for trekking
Day 05: Trek from Basntapur to Budhabare, 5hrs
Day 06: Trek from Budhabare to Guphapokhari, 5hrs
Day 07: Trek from Guphapokhari to Gorja, 5hrs
Day 08: Trek from Gorja to Dovan (put in point), 3hrs
Day 09: Rafting from Dovan to Besni Tar
Day 10: Rafting from Besni Tar to Phulbari
Day 11: Rafting from Phulbari to Esuwa Khola
Day 12: Rafting from Esuwa Khola to Nayapul
Day 13: Rafting from Nayapul to Simal Chaur
Day 14: Rafting from Simal Chaur to Tribeni Ghat
Day 15: Rating from Tribeni Ghat to Chatara, packing and drive or flight from Biratnagar to Kathmandu
