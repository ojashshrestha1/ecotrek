Ganesh Himal (Ruby Valley) Camping Trek

The Ganesh Himal Region lies in the districts of Gorkha, Nuwakot, Rasuwa and Dhading which carry historical importance within them. Ganesh Himal (Ruby Valley)Camping Trek is one of the popular trekking destinations of Nepal with magnificent views of several towering snowy peaks and valleys. This area lies within two popular destinations of Langtang National Park and Manaslu Conservation Area Project.

The most significant view includes Ganga Jamuna-twin waterfalls--Kalo-Seto and Tripurasundari temple. Beside this magnificent views Ganesh Himal (Ruby Valley) Trek is famous for its Tamang culture and beautiful isolated landscape. Ruby Valley(Kalo- Seto Kunda) Camping Trail goes through two main Tamang villages: Tangjet and Gatlang. In these villages you will get chance to observe Tamang festivals and way of their life. Moreover, you will get opportunity to be familiar with Tamang culture by staying with them at their home. In Ruby Valley Trek you will be warmly welcomed by pristine rivers, sacred active monasteries, wonderful waterfall, sweet smile of ever humble Tamang people, stunning glimpse snowy peaks and immense Himalayan landscapes adding natural and cultural gratification, delight and bliss during your trek.

Ruby Valley( Kalo- Seto Kunda) Camping Trek begins with a short drive from Kathmandu to Syabrubesi and the trail passes through awesome valley, settlement of Tamang ethnic community and reach all around Sangjung Kharka, alongside of Khola Kharka, from where you will see Ganesh Himal I (7,429m.), Ganesh Himal II (7,118m.), Ganesh III(7110m.), Ganesh IV(7,052m.), Ganesh V(6,986m.), Ganesh VII(6,350m.), Langtang Lirung (7,300m.), Manaslu (8163m.), Lamjung Himal (6800m.) ranges and many more. And the trek pass through Paldor Peak retrace back to Dhading or Gorkha where your trekking ends and finally return back to Kathmandu. There are 10 separate trekking itineraries recommended for this trek. So you can choose among these 10 trekking itineraries for your unforgettable adventure. Trekking specialist of Ecological trek have carefully designed the itineraries of Ganesh Himal Region by including adventurous route and amusing activities. While designing itineraries we tried to catch the general interest of all our trekkers.
