Everest Region

Everest Region Trekking is very popular trekking destination of Nepal. It is popular because world’s top Mount Everest 8848m is located in Everest Region. Everest Region offers the stunning glimpse of beautiful mountains such as Mt Everest (8848m.), Shishapangma (8035m.), Gosainthan (8013m.), Chooyu (8201m.), Lhotse (8516m.), Makalu (843m.), Mera Peak (6476m.), Island Peak (6189m.), Nuptse Peak (7,855m.), Amadablam (6812m.) which give breath taking experience to every trekker. On the way, there are beautiful places and villages such as Phakding, Jorsale, Namche Bazaar, Syangboche, Tengboche, Pangboche, Pheriche and Lobuche. In these places there are beautiful forests of pine, juniper and rhododendron which make long journey untried. During the trekking in the Everest region trekkers cross the several naturally decorated gadgets such as beautiful falls, peaceful lakes and pristine Rivers which will make their journey unforgettable all over of their life. Sacred monasteries, Chörtens, Gompa are scattered all over this region which will give eternal solace to the every trekker.

Himalayan Sherpa people are the main inhabitants of Khumbu region, the route goes through their villages and these people are very humble, helpful and rich in terms of unique culture and festivals. They show great hospitaility to their guests after visiting the villages of these people you have no words to appreciate their hospitaility. Sherpa ethnic people are very rich in their culture and festivals so during Everest Region Trekking you will get the opportunity to be participate in their special culture and festivals which will give you very unique experience. In short Everest Region Trekking is the unique combination of rich natural, cultural and religious aspect of the region.
