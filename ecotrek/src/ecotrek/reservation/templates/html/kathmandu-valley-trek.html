Kathmandu Valley Trek

Kathmandu – Chisapani – Nagarkot – Dhulikhel – Namobuddha is the ideal romantic trek around the Kathmandu valley. This trek takes you to all the beautiful and popular places around the Kathmandu valley. You can know the diverse Nepalese culture, lifestyle as well as can see the beautiful natural scene and scenery with flora and fauna during this trek. From Kathmandu this trek goes through the Shivapuri watershed forests and villages. The Shivapuri hill is the highest point of this trek which offer stunning glimpse of the numerous white capped peaks such as Manaslu, Buddha Himal, Ganesh Himal range, Langtang Himal range, Dorjeelakpa, Shishapangma, Gaurishankar etc. From Shivapuri hill trek we reach to Nagarkot via Chisapani. Nagarkot is very beautiful place which offer eye pleasing and heart taking natural views. Nagarkot offer the stunning views beautiful mountains, romantic views of sunrise and sunset, beautiful picture of Kathmandu valley, several green hills, and artistic view of snake like rivers and true picture of the typical Nepalese villages. After enjoying in Nagarkot with its natural beauty, trek descends to Dhulikhel. Dhulikhel also offers superb views of the beautiful mountains. Form Dhulikhel trek finally reaches to Namobuddha.

Namobuddha is the holiest place of the Buddhist pilgrimage people. According to Buddhist myth Lord Buddha from the bones and hair of the lord Buddha Namobuddha Gompa was created. In Namobuddha trekkers can enjoy with Buddhist way of mediation and prayer. During this trek along with natural beauty trekkers can get chance to know the diverse Nepalese culture because this trek passes the numerous villages of different ethnic people. Trek expert of Ecological trek has designed Kathmandu – Chisapani – Nagarkot – Dhulikhel – Namobuddha Trek itineraries. We tried our best to include all the beautiful places and romantic activities in our itineraries. We can make long or short itinerary for Kathmandu – Chisapani – Nagarkot – Dhulikhel – Namobuddha Trek as per your holidays and interest.

Day to Day Itinerary

Day 01: Arrival in Kathmandu and transfer to hotel
Day 02: 1 hr drive to Sundarijal. 5 hrs uphill trek to Chisapani and overnight at Chisapani
Day 03: 7 hrs trek to Nagarkot and overnite at Nagarkot
Day 04: Sunrise view in Nagarkot. 5 hrs trek to Dhulikhel and overnite at Dhulikhel
Day 05: Sunrise view In Dhulikhel. 1 ½ hrs drive to Namobuddha or 4 hrs trek (you can see beautiful mountain ranges and dense forest). 3 hrs trek to Panauti . Drive to Kathmandu (1 ½ hrs)
Day 06: Paragliding and other activities
Day 07: Leisure day at Kathmandu
Day 08: Final Departure
