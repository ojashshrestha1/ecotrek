Langtang Gosainkunda Helambu Trek - 16 Days

Langtang Gosainkunda Helambu Trek is a circular trek around the Langtang region that combines the Langtang valley, Gosainkunda and Helambu trek. The main attractions of this trek are Langtang valley, Helambu valley, Holy Lakes of Gosainkunda, flora and fauna of Langtang National Park. Langtang valley is the valley of glacier surrounded by White Mountain and the stunning panorama of the mountain from this valley makes you think that you are in paradise. Gosainkunda located at the altitude of 4300m is the holiest side for Hindu pilgrims. In the peak of Gosainkunda there are about dozen lakes and main are Gosainkunda, Saraswatikunda, Nagkunda and Bhairabkunda. Thousands of Hindu pilgrims trek to this place to pay homage to the lord Shiva and Gauri especially during the early autumn season. So on the way you come in touch with the Hindu pilgrims. Helambu valley is famous for its scenic beauty of Rododendron forest along with the dazzling view of mountains. Helambu is the typical village of Sherpa

Langtang Gosainkunda Helambu Trek is an interesting trek that gives the trekkers to explore the diverse people, culture and nature. Langtang Gosainkunda Helambu Trek takes you across the holy lakes of Gosainkunda, into the thick forest of Rododendron and bamboo, into the Helambu valley and Langtang valley from where you witness the eye-catching view of snow capped mountain. This scenic beauty spellbinds the trekkers with an unforgettable heavenly pleasure. It also passes through the beautiful villages of different ethnic groups like Tamang, Sherpa, Gurung which gives you opportunity to know their lifestyle and culture.

Langtang Gosainkunda Helambu Trek stars off from Syafrubesi of Rasuwa district, village located at the bank of Bhotekoshi River. From Syafrubesi you trace along Langtang Khola and ascend the uphill passing through rhododendron and bamboo forest and different settlements. On the way you pass though the unspoiled Langtang National Park, grassy downs and meadows leading to Langtang Valley and Sherpa village of Kyanjing Gompa where you experience the spiritual lifestyle of Buddhist monks in the Monastery. Then you continue your adventure to Gosainkunda, Holy site of Hindus, where you can have the sunshine in the Lakes and White Mountains and trek to Helambu and from where you return to Kathmandu.
