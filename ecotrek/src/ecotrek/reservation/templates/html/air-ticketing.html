Air Ticketing in Nepal

Air Ticketing is another invincible service that we are providing to our guests since long. Within air ticketing service we included the domestic air ticketing service, international air ticketing service and mountain flight air ticketing service. Nepal is situated in geographically difficult location and most of the naturally beautiful places are in remote areas. Most of these areas are not connected by the road and those areas which are connected by the road are also difficult to reach by bus because roads are not good, it takes long time as well as risky. In this context domestic air flight is best option to reach and visit the beautiful remote areas of Nepal. Important places of Nepal such as Jomsom, Lukla, Pokhara, Dhangadi, Jumla, Nepalgunj, Chitwan, Bhairahawa and Biratnagar are connected by the domestic airlines. Because of limited flight and bad weather it is difficult to get domestic ticket in time especially for Lukla, Jomsom and Jumla so ticket reservation should be done in time. Different Nepalese airlines such as Buddha air, Agni air, Yeti air, Sita air, Tara air and Guna air make domestic flight in these places , we will make the reservation of these airlines as per you request.

Besides the service of domestic air ticketing we will also provide the international air ticketing service. Capital city Kathmandu is connected with different Asian countries through direct international air fight. Different international airlines such as Nepal airlines, Jet airways, Thai airways, Gulf air, Silk air, Air China make the regular flight from Nepal to different Asian country such as Tibet, Bhutan, India, China, Thailand, Qatar, Singapore, Malaysia, Dubai, South Korea, Japan, Bahrain, UAE and other. We will provide ticketing service of these international air lines. Ecological trek ensure you a happy and comfortable journey without commission.
