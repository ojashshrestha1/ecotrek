Bhotekoshi River Rafting

Three hours towards the east from Kathmandu valley, the Bhotekoshi is one of the best two day rafting trip to be found in the Nepal. The usual Bhotekoshi River Rafting distance is about 95km. from Barabise to Lamosangu. Bhotekoshi, located about 70 km east of Kathmandu is snowfed from the melting Himalaya ranges. Bhotekoshi River Rafting is one of the seven most popular White-Water Rafting in Nepal. Bhotekoshi River Rafting can be divided in to two sections: upper section and lower section. The rapids at the lower section measure an overall of grade 4, while the upper section measures an incredible grade 5 to grade 6.

During the Bhotekoshi Rafting trip you can combine lots of things. Enroute to Barabise, starting point of Bhotekoshi River Rafting, there is an excellent opportunity to stop in Dhulikhel to admire astonishing views of the Himalayas.  It is also possible to combine a rafting trip on Bhotekoshi with tours of Tibet, Everest and Langtang trek. Moreover, one of the world’s deepest gorge bungee jumps is based in this River’s gorge. So, you can also have fun of it. This is the steepest River rafted in Nepal. The River is very popular among rafting enthusiasts. The best time for rafting along this River is from October to December and from February to April.

Itinerary

Day 01: Arrival in Kathmandu and transfer to Hotel
Day 02: Sightseeing of Kathmandu valley
Day 03: 5hrs drive from Kathmandu to Lamosangu, put in point (95 km east to Kathmandu) and camping
Day 04: 5hrs rafting from Lamosangu to Dolalghat (put out point) and drive back toKathmandu
