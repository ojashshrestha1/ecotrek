Singuchuli Peak Climbing

Singuchuli also known as fluted peak is another beautiful peak located in Annapurna region. During your journey you will feel that you are in nature paradise because Annapurna region is considered world best place in terms of Natural beauty. SinguChuli is considered one of the difficult peaks for climbing in comparison to other peaks of Nepal. It is above 600m peak with heavy ice fall so it demands good physical fitness along with good snowy peaks climbing experience. There are more routes to climb Singuchuli but the usual climbing route is the northeast face between the glacier and the flank of the mountain to reach the plateau below the northeast face. In order to triumph singuchuli summit our journey starts with 6/7 hours bus drive from Kathmandu to Pokhara. From Phedi we will reach to Singuchuli base camp after completing natural adventurous journey of many beautiful places such as Tolka, Jhinu, Dovan, Machhapuchhre base camp and Annapurna base camp. From Singuchuli Base Camp we will finally reach to its summit. Singuchuli summit offer great view of mountains such as Annapurna, Machhapuchhre, Dhaulagiri, Nilgiri, Tharpuchuli, Himchuli etc. after rewarding this beautiful mountain trip return through Chomrong, Ghorepani Hill route. And these places are famous for Gurung culture and romantic scenery so SinguChuli journey is unique combination of beautiful natural and important culture things. Ecological trek has been designed Singuchuli peak expedition itineraries by our Himalaya trekking expert. We tried our best to include adventurous trail and interesting activity in our itineraries. If our itineraries do not suit for you then we are ready to make itineraries long or short in accordance to your desire and holiday time

Day to Day Itinerary

Day 01: Meeting upon arrival at Kathmandu International Airport, transfer to hotel.
Day 02: Departure for Pokhara, overnight at hotel.
Day 03: Trek to Tolka, overnight at guesthouse.
Day 04: Trek to Jhinu, overnight at guesthouse.
Day 05: Trek to Dovan, overnight at guesthouse.
Day 06: Junbesi to Nuntala (2440m), 5/6 hours of walk.
Day 07: Trek to Annapurna base camp, overnight at guesthouse.
Day 08: TharpuChuli base camp, overnight at tented camp.
Day 09: SinguChuli base camp, overnight at tented camp.
Day 10: High camp SinguChuli, overnight at tented camp.
Day 11: Summit SinguChuli and back to base camp, overnight at tented camp.
Day 12: Trek to Deurali, overnight at guesthouse.
Day 13: Trek to Chhomrong, overnight at guesthouse.
Day 14: Trek to Tadapani, overnight at guesthouse.
Day 15: Trek to Ghorepani (Poonhill), overnight at guesthouse.
Day 16: Trek to Hile, overnight at guesthouse.
Day 17: Trek to Nayapul and drive back to Pokhara, overnight at hotel.
Day 18: Drive back to Kathmandu, overnight at hotel.
Day 19: Rest at Kathmandu, overnight at hotel.
Day 20: Transfer to airport, departure on your own destination.
