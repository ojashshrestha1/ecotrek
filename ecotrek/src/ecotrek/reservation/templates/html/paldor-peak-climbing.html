Paldor Peak Climbing

Paldor Peak is located in Ganesh Himal Region which is also popular trekking destination of Nepal. Beside magnificent views of several towering snowy peaks Ganesh Himal Region is famous for its Tamang culture and beautiful isolated landscape. Our trail goes through two main Tamang villages: Tangjet and Gatlang. In these villages you will get chance to observe Tamang festivals and their way of life. Moreover you will get opportunity to be familiar with Tamang culture by staying with them in their home. Paldor is below 6000m peak so this expedition is best for those who want to gain necessary experience for top mountain expedition. Any interested physically fit climbers easily can go Paldor Peak Expedition. Our journey towards Paldor peak summit starts from Kathmandu. After completing 6/7 hours bus journey we will reach to Dhunche. From Dhunche we will move forward by crossing different places such as syabruGaon, Thaugjet, Yuri Kharka, Lari Mine and reach to Paldor Base Camp. Finally from base camp we will reach to Paldor summit. From Paldor summit magnificent picture of Ganesh Himal, Pabil, Mt. Langtang, Mt. Manaslu, LgosangKarpo, Ganesh Himal I (7,406), Ganesh Himal V (6.950 m), and Langtang valley can be seen. After rewarding these magnificent pictures, trip returns via same route to Kathmandu. Ecological trek has been designed Paldor Peak Expedition itineraries by our Himalaya trekking expert. We tried our best to include adventurous trail and interesting activity in our itineraries. If our itineraries do not suit for you then we are ready to make itineraries long or short in accordance to your desire and holiday time.
