Simikot - Mt Kailash - Everest Base Camp - Lhasa Tour

Simikot – Kailash – EBC – Lhasa Trek is another ideal tour in the Major places of Nepal and Tibet. This tour starts from Simikot after taking the short flight from Nepalgunj. This extraordinary journey takes you through the hidden Humla valley in Nepal's Midwestern part. Simikot is the gateway to the mount Kailash and Lake Manasarowor. From Simikot we will reach to mount Kailash by observing beautiful landscapes and Tibetan lifestyle. Mount Kailash is the main destination of this tour package after completing the romantic journey of several beautiful places such as DharaPokhari, Salli Khola, Muchu, Sipsip, Hilsa, Taklakot, Manasarowor, Tarchen, DiraPuk and Dolma-La Pass we will reach to Mount Kailash. Mount Kailash is the religiously significant place for the people related to three religions: Hindu, Buddhist and Jain. And it is the sources of three mighty rivers: Indus, Brahmaputra and Karnali Tibetan Buddhists believe Kang Rinpoche, which means Precious Snow Mountain, is a natural Mandala representing the Buddhist cosmology on the earth and the Janis believe that this is the place where their religion's founder was spiritually awakened.

In Hinduism, it is considered to be the abode of Lord Shiva and a place of eternal bliss. After visiting Kailash Mountain we will go to Everest base camp through Tarchen, Saga and Rongbuk. Everest base camp offer the stunning glimpse of the beautiful surrounding mountains. After appreciating the beautiful scenery in Everest base camp we will go to Lhasa by observing beautiful Tibetan landscapes, stunning mountains, Monasteries and lifestyle through Namta Pasto, Zemukh, Tsojan, Old Tingri, Shakya Monastery and Gyantse. In Lhasa we will visit the holiest of Tibetan Temple Jokhang and the nearby traditional market place of Bharkhor bazaar. Watching the Tibetan pilgrims who come to this temple from all over Tibet is a great experience and gives an insight on the religious Tibetan life. Our tour also covers the famous Potala and Norbulingka Palace of the Dalai Lamas which are situated in Chengguan district and other important places such as Lhasa Zhol Pillar, Chokpori Hill, and Lingkhor Path. Finally we will take flight from Lhasa to Kathmandu. Tour expert of Ecological trek has designed Simikot – Kailash – EBC – Lhasa Tour itineraries. We tried our best to include all the important places in our itineraries. If you are interested to visit other places which are not included in our package we will redesign our itinerary of Simikot – Kailash – EBC – Lhasa Tour as per your request.
