Tamang Heritage Treks

Tamang Heritage Trekking trail offers the combined experience of the culture and tradition of the local Tamang people and the natural beauty as well as the beauty of mountains. It is the recently opened trekking route in the Langtang region towards the north of Kathmandu valley. Langtang region is the land of magic, meditation, and shamanism. It is the best idea to explore your holiday in Nepal. Tamang heritage trekking route provides the opportunity to explore the culture, religion and daily lifestyle of Tamang community of Langtang region. Tamang are the descendent of the Tibetan people and have hardly seen any tourist as well as their culture isvirtually untouched. The hearty welcome of the Local people, their rich culture, beautiful ancient monasteries, and unique architect of their houses allures everyone who goes for the Tamang heritage trek. The gorgeous landscapes, alpine meadows, large yak and sheep grazing pastures, forest of rhododendron, terraced hills adds further pleasure to the trekkers.

The Tamang heritage trekking in the Langtang region commences from Safrubeshi of Rasuwa district. In this trek you pass through the different Tamang settlements like Goljung, Gatlang, Chilime, Thuman, Timure and Briddim. During the trek you can see the panoramic view of the mountains like Lantang, Kerung, Ganesh Himal, Sanjen Himal ranges etc. which will be unforgettable. Furthermore you can also visit local monasteries of DukchuGomba which is chosen as sacred site by the great Tibetan Lama, Guru Rinpoche and ChuklaKhnagaGomba. In route you can take bath in the natural spring of hot water at Tatopani which is believed to cure the skin diseases. Tamang heritage trek offers you the life time memory and in spring and autumn season you to get full enjoyment of the nature and culture of this trekking trail.
