Annapurna Round with ABC Trek - 26 Days

Annapurna Region is artistically decorated with natural beauty and considered nature oasis. Because of this fact Annapurna Round Trekking is considered one of the best treks of the world. There are four regions that are passed through on the Annapurna round trek; Lamjung, Manang, Mustang and Myagdi. Lamjung and Myagdi are located in the lower elevation with beautiful lush landscape. Manang and Mustang are located in higher elevation with beautiful snowy peaks and barren landscape. Annapurna Region offer the stunning glimpse of beautiful snowy peaks such as Annapurna, Machhapuchhre, Dhaulagiri, Nilgiri, Manaslu, Buddha Himal, Himchuli, Ganesh Himal, Dhampus peak, Tukuche Peak, Thorung Peak and Tilicho Peak. The trail goes through the beautiful forest, falls, Lake, pristine River and artistic landscape which catches the eye and take the heart of the trekkers.

On the way, there are Brahmin, Chhetri, Gurung, Magar and Tibetan Buddhist villages. These ethnic people are very rich in their culture and festivals so during this trek trekkers get opportunity to know their culture and participate in their festivals. Sacred temples, Gompa and ch√∂rtens are scattered all over the region which will give ultimate solace to the trekkers. Annapurna Round with ABC Trek starts from Besisahar and ends in Nayapul. It goes all the beautiful places of the region such as Besisahar, Bahundada, Dharapani, Chame, Pisang, Manang, Yak kharka, Throng Phedi, Muktinath, Jomsom, Marpha, Ghasa, Tatopani, Ghorepani, Poon hill, Chhomrong, Bamboo, Deaurali and reach to Annapurna Base Camp, after enjoying in the peace natural environment in Annapurna Base Camp it will descend to Nayapul. The route goes past the Thorung La Pass 5416m, Manaslu, Langtang Himal, Annapurna I, Annapurna II and IV, Annapurna III and Gangapurna, and Dhaulagiri. This trek passes through the world's deepest gorge in between two 8000m above peaks Annapurna and Dhaulagiri.
