Annapurna Region

Annapurna Region is artistically decorated with natural beauty and considered nature oasis. Because of this fact Annapurna Region Trekking is considered one of the best trekking destinations of the world. There are four region that are passed through the Annapurna Region Trek; Lamjung, Manang, Mustang and Myagdi. Lamjung and Myagdi are located in the lower elevation with beautiful lush landscape. Manang and Mustang are located in higher elevation with beautiful snowy peaks and barren landscape. Annapurna region offers the stunning glimpse of beautiful snowy peaks such as Annapurna, Machhapuchhre, Dhaulagiri, Nilgiri, Manaslu, Buddha Himal, Himchuli, Ganesh Himal, Dhampus peak, Tukuche peak, Thorang peak and Tilicho peak. The trail goes through the beautiful forest, falls, Lake, pristine River and artistic landscape which catches the eye and takes the heart of the trekkers. On the way there are Brahmin, Chhetri, Gurung, Magar and Tibetan Buddhist villages on the way. These ethnic people are very rich in their culture and festivals, so during this trek trekkers get opportunity to know their culture and participate in their festivals. There are long and short trek trips available in the Annapurna Region. The long trek is also called Annapurna Circuit Trek which goes counter-clockwise from Besisahar to Nayapul and reaches its high point in Thorang La (pass) at the height of 5416m. The route goes past the Manaslu, Langtang Himal, Annapurna I, Annapurna II and IV, Annapurna III and Gangapurna, and Dhaulagiri. This trek pass through the World's Deepest Gorge in between two 8000m above peaks Annapurna and Dhaulagiri and finally reach to Poon hill which offers sunrise, sunset and beautiful mountains views. Famous short treks of this region are Panchase Lake trek, Royal Trek, Sarangkot Trek, Ghorepani Trek, Siklis Trek, Ghandruk Trek and Poon Hill Trek. Most of them are started from in and around the Pokhara.

Annapurna Base Camp Trekking, Annapurna Circuit Trek, Jomsong Muktinath Trek ,Ghorepani Poon Hill Trek , Royal Trek, Siklis Trek, Mardi Himal, Dhampus Sarangkot Trek, Tilicho Lake Trek and Narphu Valley Trek are the major attraction of Trekking in Annapurna Region.
