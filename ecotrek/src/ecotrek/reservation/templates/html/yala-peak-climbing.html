Yala Peak Climbing - 15 Days

Yala Peak (5732m) is located in the Langtang Region, around 20 miles north of Kathmandu valley. Yala Peak is the easiest peak to climb and it is best suited for beginner mountaineers and trekkers. Yala Peak Climbing gives you the opportunity to take experience of the mountaineering in short period of time. It offers the amazing view of the snowcapped mountains like Langtang Lirung, Dorje Lakpa Himal, Lenpo Gang,Gangchempo etc.The Buddhist monasteries, Chortens and Mani Walls with beautiful colorful prayer flags, the huge pastures, glaciers and desolate Langtang alpine valley, cultural diversities are some of the attraction of this trekking and peak climbing. This is the easiest peak climbing and previous climbing experience is not necessary but needs physical fitness. To reach the base camp, you pass through the thick forest, different ethnic villages etc.

After 6-7 hour drive from Kathmandu you reach Syaprubeshi which is the starting point of for the Yala Peak Climbing and trekking. From Syaprubeshi the trekking trail of the Yala Peak Climbing goes uphill passing through the forest of Rhododendron, oak and bamboo, small settlement of Tamang community which is a chance for you to explore the culture and life style of them which is similar to the Tibet culture, Langtang National Park where you may encounter the wild animals like red panda, snow leopard, wild boar etc. after you reach Langtang valley, which is also known as the valley of glacier, you headed to Kyanjin Gompa at the elevation of 3800m where you take rest for two or three days from acclimatization. During these days you can hike to different spectacular places to view the amazing panorama of the mountain. From there you walk to the base camp and climb the Yala Peak from where you observe the beautiful sceneries of Langtang Lirung other mountains, glaciers etc. after you climb the peak you descend to the base camp and return back to Kathmandu via same route. This trek will be one of the notable trekking in your life.

Day to Day Itinerary

Day 01: Arrival in Kathmandu airport
Day 02: Pre-trip Meeting and Kathmandu Valley Sightseeing
Day 03: Drive to SybruBensi (1462meters) by bus 8/9 hours
Day 04: Trek to Lama Hotel (2500meters) 5/6 hours
Day 05: Trek to Langtang Village (3307meters) 5/6 hours
Day 06: Trek to Kyanjin Gompa (3798meters) 4/5 hours
Day 07: Kyanjin Gompa exploration and acclimatization day
Day 08: Trek to Yala Peak Base Camp (4400meters) 5/6 hours
Day 09: Summit Yala Peak (5732 meters) and back to Kyangjin Gompa.
Day 10: Spare day in case of bad weather
Day 11: Trek back to Lama Hotel (2500meters) 5/6 hours
Day 12: Trek to SyabruBesi (1462meters) 6/7 hours
Day 13: Drive back to Kathmandu by bus. 8/9 hours
Day 14: Leisure day in Kathmandu for valley sightseeing and shopping
Day 15: Final Departure
